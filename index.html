For Learners: Get Exactly What You Need

Stop wasting time on endless video playlists. Tell us what you want to learn, who you want to learn from, and we’ll deliver it. On your terms.

Maybe you’ve always wanted to understand how Duolingo built a habit-forming streak system, so you ask the designer who created it. Or you’re building your first startup and want real advice, so you connect with a founder who scaled their product from 0 to 50,000 users. These aren’t random videos or recycled talks. They’re real conversations built around your questions, with the people who’ve actually done the work. One request can give you (and so many others for decades) insight, direction, and access that changes everything.
