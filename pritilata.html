Smart vending and eco-solutions for menstrual health in universities and workplaces across Bangladesh.

Pritilata is dedicated to making periods easier for women. Convenient availability of menstrual products through smart vending machines at schools and workplaces where one can simply grab what they want without any hassle. Our app helps women to place orders for products, track their period cycles, and gain health advice using an AI chatbot. We are also creating eco-friendly interventions such as recycling pads and breast health aids. Starting with universities, we’re growing step by step to reach more women, especially where period products are hard to get. Basically, Pritilata is here to break the stigma, make periods easier, and keep things green, all while supporting women’s health.
