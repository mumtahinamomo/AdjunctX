Nirbhoya

Empowering women in remote communities through menstrual, mental, and reproductive health education and technology.

Nirbhoya is a women-led initiative transforming menstrual and reproductive health in Bangladesh, particularly targeting remote and marginalized communities. Millions of women lack essential awareness related to their menstrual, mental and reproductive health, related resources, and affordable hygiene products; Nirbhoya bridges these gaps through education, economic empowerment, and technology.

Our activities include community-based awareness sessions, having reached nearly 3,000 tea worker women through 40 courtyard meetings in Sreemangal led by medical professionals. Concurrently, Nirbhoya is researching menstrual and reproductive health practices in tea estates to design impactful interventions.

Our sustainable social enterprise empowers marginalized women to produce reusable sanitary pads, with proceeds supporting education for girls in tea garden communities.
