Curriculum-specific AI Tutor tailored for K–12 students in Bangladesh.

Askies AI is a curriculum-specific AI Tutor for K–12 students in Bangladesh, currently operating in stealth mode. The platform is being designed to align with NCTB textbooks and educational standards, helping students get personalized, on-demand assistance for core subjects. With a mission to make quality education universally accessible, Askies AI is focused on building a highly contextual, culturally relevant tool that blends AI-powered tutoring with real academic outcomes.
