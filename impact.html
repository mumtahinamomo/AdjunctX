Our Impact

Till the end of May 2025, our solution has served 92 learners, and enabled 3 instructors in deep tech, climate & sustainability, and mental health to share their expertise in a way they had not done before. Many of our learners are entrepreneurs running their own ventures, who have collectively raised around US $68,500 in grants and investments.

Here are some of the ventures our learners have started:

Envirocove

Founder: Sihab Bin Toriq

A multi-vendor marketplace connecting 600+ vendors with 32,000+ repair businesses across Bangladesh to enable affordable, reusable electronics and reduce e-waste.

Pritilata

Founder: Suraiya Tasnur

Making periods easier and eco-friendly through smart vending machines, pad recycling, AI-based health chatbots, and a growing app-based community.

Calcifer

Founder: Ilham Foysal

A creative platform using aesthetic, nature-based videos to inspire mindfulness, inner peace, and meaningful reflection through visual storytelling.

Askies AI

Founder: Tamzid Rahman

A curriculum-specific AI tutor built for K-12 students in Bangladesh to enhance foundational learning and access to national education content.

Nirbhoya

Founder: Shahidul Islam Khan Jisan

A social venture improving menstrual and reproductive health in marginalized communities through education, community health tech, and eco-friendly pads.

Mark My Water

Founder: Tasnuva Helal Bidita

A data-driven platform optimizing water usage across agriculture and industry by providing real-time analytics to mitigate global water scarcity.
