A data-driven platform to revolutionize global water management and empower sustainable practices (Backed by Oxfam).

Mark My Water (MMW) is a data-driven platform revolutionizing water management. The platform provides innovative solutions to address global water scarcity by empowering farmers, businesses, and governments with real-time data and actionable insights.

MMW technology optimizes water use, reduces waste, and promotes sustainable practices across industries. By combining advanced monitoring tools, smart analytics, and user-friendly dashboards, it enables smarter decisions in agriculture, urban planning, and climate resilience.

With a mission to build a water-secure future, MMW is not just about technology — it’s about equipping communities and institutions with the tools they need to preserve our planet’s most precious resource.
